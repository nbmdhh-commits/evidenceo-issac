Myocardial Infarction Pathway Algorithm

1. Symptoms Presenting

Crushing chest pain radiating to left arm, diaphoresis.

Checkpoint Question

What is the priority clinical window for ECG?

↓
2. ECG Acquisition

Verify ST-segment elevations or T-wave inversions.

↓
3. Biomarker Testing

Troponin I/T, CK-MB enzyme assays.

↓
4. Risk Stratification

STEMI, NSTEMI, or unstable angina classification.

↓
5. Reperfusion / Medical Management

PCI, thrombolytic therapy, loading DAPT (antiplatelets, anticoagulants).
